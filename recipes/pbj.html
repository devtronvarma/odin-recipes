Banana Peanut Butter Sandwich

Description

This one's a simple and easy recipe that I go back to time and again when I want something tasty and fast. What was once just a humble peanut butter and jelly sandwich is now something more gourmet and refined. The awesome thing is that it still only takes about 10-15 minutes to make! This one goes great with a cup of coffee (especially during a late night coding session).

Ingredients

  • Peanut butter (either crunchy or smooth)
  • Almond butter
  • 1 medium banana, sliced
  • 2 slices sprouted wheat bread
  • 3-4 walnuts (optional)
  • Cinnamon (optional)
  • Honey or maple syrup (optional)

Steps

  1. Spread the peanut & almond butter mixture on both slices of bread, and top one half with sliced bananas.
  2. Chop 3-4 walnuts, and add on top of the banana slices along with a sprinkle of cinnamon and a drizzle of honey
  3. Top with the other slice of bread (that already has peanut/almond butter spread on it), and place sandwiches in the panini press (or grill sandwiches on a grill pan over medium heat if you do not have a panini press).
  4. Once the top has browned and sides of the sandwiches are slightly crispy, remove from the sandwich press and let cool for 1-2 min. Cut your gourmet banana peanut butter sandwich in half diagonally and serve warm or at room temperature.
